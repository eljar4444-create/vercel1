# Validation Strategy: Phase 3.1

## Dimension 8: Validation Mechanisms

**Metrics / Triggers:**
- Ensure `/search` requests do not return providers who lack an avatar or service.

**Post-Execution Validation:**
- Go to dashboard of test user.
- Try to click "Publish" without photo. Validate failure.
- Try to click "Publish" without service. Validate failure.
- Complete both, click Publish. Confirm profile flips to `PUBLISHED` and alert disappears.
